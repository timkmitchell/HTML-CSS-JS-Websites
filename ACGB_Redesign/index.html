About ACG

AC Grooming has been in business for over 21 years with previous experience working as a veterinarian's assistant for 30 years.

The business was established in 1996 by Tony C.. In 2016 he passed the business to his sons along with the experience and knowledge to continue his legacy.

Our business specializes in pet grooming, particularly, dogs and cats, ranging from small, medium to large. Boarding is available for both dogs and cats.
